Object of Game

Sea Battle is a 2 players game where players take turns trying to destroy the other player's fleet of ships on a 10x10 grid:

The game is played in 2 phases: 1. Ship Placement Phase and 2. Attack Phase

Ship Placement Phase

At the start of each game, you must place your ships on the grid.

Each player starts out with 6 ships.

To place a ship, you can click and drag a ship to available area.

You can rotate a ship by simply double click on it.

you can also click Random button to randomly assign placement to each ship.

Once you've finished placing your ships, click on the Ready button.

Attack Phase

When both players have clicked on their Ready button, the Attack Phase will now start.

Both players will now see thr following game board:

The larger grid represents your opponent's are. The smaller grid represents your area and your ships.

When it is your turn, click on the square on the main grid you wish to attack.

Every time you hit an enemy ship, you get another turn. When you miss,tour turn is over.

when you've sunk your opponent's ship, that ship will appear on the main grid.

The first person to sink all five of his/her opponent's ships wins the games.

Game Rules

The objective in Dots & Boxes is to capture more boxes on the board than your opponent.

Each player takes turn connecting a line between 2 adjacent dots either horizontally or vertically. Simple click between 2 available dots to create a line between them.

Capturing a Box

When you place the 4th line that completes a box, that box will be captured by you, regardless whether any of the other lines were created by your opponent.

Endgame

The game end when there are no more dots to connect.

The player that has captured the most boxes wins the game.
